How Can You Say That It Is Possible?

Have you experience good eye days where you can see a lot better than yesterday?. Do you experience a little blur in your vision when your tired or hungry or not feeling well? That only means that our vision changes and our eyes are adoptive muscles.

Good thing our eyes can change and adopt to whatever we do. Think of it as a muscle that needs to train in order to get stronger and stronger.
