This is how I do my daily outdoor routine when I go to work. I just look at the cars passing by and trying to focus on each plate numbers while its moving.

Im doing active focus by doing this routine but when doing this, also notice your peripheral , it means while looking at the plate number also be aware of the objects around it like the people on the street, sign boards, trees , cars & etc. Peripheral is what helps you to focus on the plate numbers.
