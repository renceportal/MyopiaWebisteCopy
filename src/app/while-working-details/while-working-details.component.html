You might think looking at the computer for hours will not help you to reverse your nearsightedness and might as well make it worse?. Your probably right and wrong..

Why your right?

People used to looking at their computer or even smart phones with not so near distance, this makes the image or text on the screen so clear that makes your eyes elongated, this makes the image from the front lens of your eye going to the front of the retina, not on the back of the retina which is the normal phase.

Also if your looking at your screen and not aware of your peripheral, this makes your ciliary muscles spasm. means your eyes are now lock up when looking at distance or near objects. Coz ciliary muscle changes when we look at things up close and up front.

Why your wrong?

You can train your eyes while looking at your screen by doing the reverse. Simply adjust your screen distance until you see a little bit of blur challenge on the image or text. Also be aware of your peripheral like your seatmate, cup beside your computer, people walking around, people in your front and etc..
